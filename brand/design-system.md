## Logo

- Always write: **GoTixi** (capital G, capital T — never "go tixi", "GOTIXI", "Go-Tixi")
- Light logo (cream) on dark/Forest Green backgrounds: `https://gotixi.com/images/common/logo-dark.svg`

Duck mascot 🦆: use in onboarding, community, celebration contexts. Never in formal/financial copy.

## Voice & Tone

**GoTixi speaks like a knowledgeable friend, not a corporate agent.**

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| "We handle the details. You create the memories." | "Book NOW for AMAZING deals!" |
| "Explore independently, travel confidently." | "Solo travel? No problem! Super affordable!" |
| "Refined travel, intelligently planned." | "Luxury trips at lowest guaranteed rates. Limited time!" |
| "Support before, during, and if plans change." | "24/7 customer service for queries and complaints." |

- First-person plural: "We curate…", "Our team…", "We've got you"
- Confident, calm — never shouting
- No ALL CAPS in body, no exclamation overload
- Never lead with price/discounts — lead with quality, care, peace of mind
- Never call GoTixi a "booking platform" or "OTA" — we are a **travel partner**

## Core Brand Values (for all ad copy)

1. **Warm** — every touchpoint feels caring, not processed
2. **Safe** — we remove fear (visas, unknown destinations, logistics)
3. **Effortless** — we absorb complexity so travelers don't have to
4. **Human-led, AI-assisted** — lead with the human element
5. **Deeply Personal, Never Templated**
6. **Balanced Over Bargain** — quality and curation, not cheapest

## Photography Direction

- **Destination over product** — evocative places, not hotel lobbies
- **Warm light** — golden hour, dusk, naturally warm tones (no cold/desaturated)
- **People as presence** — candid, absorbed in experience (no posed tourist smiles)
- **Authentic local texture** — markets, architecture, landscapes
- **Crop ratios:** Cards = 3:2 landscape · Hero = full-bleed or 16:9

---

## Positioning

- Not an OTA, not a booking engine
- "Built by travelers, not templates."
- vs. MakeMyTrip/Yatra: we curate, not aggregate; quality over volume
- vs. Traditional agents: warmth + modern speed + transparency
- vs. DIY: "you decide; we execute" — time and peace of mind over savings
