Brand Purpose & Mission

Why GoTixi exists

Every brand decision flows from one belief: international travel should feel warm — not overwhelming. Real human expertise, backed by smart technology, makes that possible.

"Making International Travel Warm, Safe and Effortless."

— the promise behind every trip we plan
🌡️

Warm

Every interaction — message, itinerary, or check-in call — should leave the traveler feeling cared for, not processed.

🛡️

Safe

From complex visas to unknown destinations, we remove fear. Safety is the confidence we give every customer before they leave home.

✨

Effortless

We absorb the complexity so travelers don't have to. The best trip is one where nothing feels like work — because we've done it all for them.

"Great journeys don't come from algorithms or templates. They're crafted through real travel experience, careful judgment, and an understanding of what truly makes a trip unforgettable."

— GoTixi, gotixi.com

02

Logo & Identity

The GoTixi mark

The logo is our most recognisable asset. Use the correct version for each background, never alter it, and always give it room to breathe.

Light logo — for dark backgrounds
Dark logo — for light backgrounds
Always

Name Casing

"GoTixi" — one word, capital G, capital T. Never "go tixi", "GOTIXI", "Go-Tixi", or "Gotixi". The casing reflects intentionality: it's both a verb and a destination.

Always

Clear Space

Maintain space equal to the cap-height of "G" on all four sides. Never crowd the logo with competing text, imagery, or graphic noise.

Never

Logo Alterations

Do not stretch, rotate, recolor, drop-shadow, or modify the logo file. Always use the official SVG provided. When in doubt, request the asset from the brand team.

The Duck Mascot 🦆

Customers are "Ducklings" — a term of warmth and community. The duck softens, never diminishes. Use it in onboarding, community contexts, and celebrations. Never in formal legal or financial copy.

04

Typography

Words that land well

Four typefaces, each with a specific job. Together they create a layered voice: editorial authority from Volkhov, clean clarity from Poppins, structural precision from Inter, and human warmth from Caveat.

Display / Headlines — Volkhov (Serif)

Journeys Crafted
With Care, Not Clicks.

Weights: 400 Regular · 700 Bold · Italic variants  |  Use: H1 heroes, H2 section titles, pull quotes, destination names

Body / UI — Poppins (Sans-serif)

From planning complex itineraries to key moments on the road, every GoTixi trip is guided by thoughtful human judgment — with support when it counts. Smart technology for speed and precision, with every trip reviewed by experienced travel experts.

Weights: 300 Light for body · 400 Regular for UI · 500 Medium for buttons  |  Use: body copy, buttons, card text, nav items

Labels / Captions — Inter (Sans-serif)

Travel Guide · Visa Free · 6 Days · Direct Flights · Summer Special

Weights: 400 · 500  |  Use: overlines, tags, small labels, URLs, table headers — always uppercase, letter-spacing: 3–4px

Accent — Caveat (Handwritten)

"We handle the details. You create the memories." ✈︎

Weights: 400 · 500  |  Use: taglines, signature phrases, newsletter headers — sparingly; one instance per layout maximum

Typographic Scale

H1 Hero: Volkhov 56–92px, 400 · H2 Section: Volkhov 32–50px · H3 Card: Poppins 16–18px, 500 · Body: Poppins 14–16px, 300 · Label: Inter 10–11px, 500, ALL CAPS, letter-spacing 3px · Accent: Caveat 22–36px, 500

Core Brand Values

What we stand for

These values describe how GoTixi already operates — not aspirations. They should be evident in every product decision, communication, and customer interaction.

Human-led, AI-assisted

Technology accelerates but never replaces judgment. Every GoTixi trip is reviewed by an experienced human. In all customer-facing communications, lead with the human element — AI is the engine, not the face.

Deeply Personal, Never Templated

We don't do copy-paste itineraries. Our brand should never feel mass-produced — not in copy, design, or service. Every traveler's uniqueness should feel acknowledged from the first message to the final check-in.

With You at Every Step

GoTixi's 24/7 support is not a feature — it's a core value. Presence and reliability should permeate our voice, response times, and the feeling our brand leaves behind long after the trip ends.

Balanced Over Bargain

We never compete on being cheapest. Our position is quality, curation, and peace of mind. Brand communications should never lead with discounts. Price is secondary to rightness, fit, and the value of a stress-free trip.

Photography Style

Images that feel like arriving

GoTixi photography should make the viewer feel the warmth of landing somewhere new. Real light, real places, real moments — not staged stock perfection.

1

Destination over Product

Show destinations at their most evocative — golden-hour streets, water textures, architectural detail. Never lead with hotel lobbies or generic "happy tourist" stock imagery.

2

Warm Light Preference

Favor golden hour, dusk, or naturally warm tones. Cold, desaturated, or heavily filtered imagery contradicts our "Warm" value. Our world should feel inviting, not clinical.

3

People as Presence, Not Pose

When humans appear, they should feel candid — absorbed in the experience. Travel is about being present; photos should capture that state, not performative smiles for the camera.

4

Authentic Local Texture

Prefer images of genuine local culture — markets, architecture, landscapes — over chain hotel rooms or airport terminals. We sell places, not logistics.

5

Consistent Crop Ratios

Destination cards use a fixed 3:2 landscape crop. Hero images are full-bleed or 16:9. Never use portrait-oriented images in card grids — consistency builds visual trust across site and ads.

Positioning & Differentiation

Where GoTixi stands

GoTixi is not a booking engine, not an OTA. It occupies a distinct position: the expert friend with 24/7 access — human-led, AI-assisted, and deeply personal.

"Built by travelers, not templates."

— the line that separates us from every OTA

vs. OTAs (MakeMyTrip, Yatra, EaseMyTrip)

OTAs optimize for transaction volume. GoTixi optimizes for trip quality. We are curators, not aggregators. Our edge is human judgment and care — not lowest price or widest inventory.

vs. Traditional Travel Agents

Traditional agents have relationships but often lack transparency and modern speed. GoTixi brings AI efficiency and clean UX while keeping the warmth that platforms have stripped away.

vs. DIY Travelers

DIY travelers trade stress for savings. GoTixi exists for those who value their time and peace of mind. We remove complexity without removing control — "you decide; we execute."

The Irreducible Promise

Every GoTixi customer should feel they have a trusted expert in their corner — before, during, and after the trip. This commitment is the differentiator no algorithm can replicate.

Brand Do's & Don'ts

Usage guardrails

Consistency is built through small decisions. These rules protect the brand experience across every touchpoint — social, email, ads, proposals, and in-person interactions.

✓ Always Do
Write "GoTixi" — capital G and capital T — in all contexts
Use Volkhov for all headline and display-level text
Use Sunset Orange (#F08802) for all primary CTAs and brand accents
Use Forest Green (#323729) for dark backgrounds, headers, and footers
Apply Caveat font for taglines and signature phrases — max one per layout
Reference the 3 core values (Warm, Safe, Effortless) in all pitches
Lead with human expertise over technology in all customer-facing copy
Use the correct logo version — light on dark, dark on light backgrounds
Use warm-toned, 3:2 crop destination photography in card grids
Speak in first-person plural — "We curate…", "Our team…", "We've got you"
✕ Never Do
Write "go tixi", "GOTIXI", "Go-Tixi", or "gotixi" in any branded context
Use Arial, Roboto, or Inter as a headline typeface in brand materials
Substitute brand orange with platform-default blue or any purple gradient
Stretch, rotate, recolor, shadow, or modify the GoTixi logo in any way
Use Caveat for body copy, buttons, or functional UI text
Lead with price as the primary value proposition ("cheapest packages")
Describe GoTixi as a "booking platform" or "OTA" — we are a travel partner
Use cold, desaturated, or heavily filtered photography in brand materials
Use ALL CAPS in body copy or overuse exclamation marks in any copy
Publish generic templated itineraries with no personalization signals
