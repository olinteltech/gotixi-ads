# GoTixi Brand Guidelines

When working on any ad, creative, copy, or design task in this repo, load and apply the GoTixi brand system. Here is the complete reference:

---

## Mission
**"Making International Travel Warm, Safe and Effortless."**

GoTixi is a human-led, AI-assisted travel partner for Indian international travelers. Customers are called **Ducklings**. Entry price point: ₹75,000. We are NOT an OTA or booking platform — we are a travel partner.

## Logo

- Name: always **GoTixi** — capital G, capital T. Never "go tixi", "GOTIXI", "Go-Tixi".
- Light logo (on dark): `https://gotixi.com/images/common/logo-dark.svg`
- Dark logo (on light): `https://gotixi.com/images/common/logo.svg`
- Never stretch, rotate, recolor, or add drop shadows.

Duck mascot 🦆: use in onboarding, community, celebration. Never in formal/financial copy.

---

## Voice & Tone

GoTixi speaks like a knowledgeable friend — confident, warm, calm. Never hype, never corporate.

**Do:**
- "We handle the details. You create the memories."
- "Explore independently, travel confidently."
- "Refined travel, intelligently planned."
- "Support before you take off, while you're traveling, and if plans change."
- First-person plural: "We curate…", "Our team…", "We've got you"

**Never:**
- "Book NOW for AMAZING deals!"
- ALL CAPS in body copy
- Exclamation overload
- Lead with price or discounts
- Call GoTixi a "booking platform" or "OTA"

## Core Values (reference in all ad copy)

1. **Warm** — every touchpoint feels caring, not processed
2. **Safe** — we remove fear around visas, unknowns, logistics
3. **Effortless** — we absorb complexity so travelers don't have to
4. **Human-led, AI-assisted** — lead with the human element
5. **Deeply Personal, Never Templated**
6. **Balanced Over Bargain** — never compete on cheapest

## Photography Direction

- Destination over product (no hotel lobbies, no generic stock tourists)
- Warm light — golden hour, dusk, naturally warm tones
- People as presence — candid, absorbed, not posed
- Authentic local texture — markets, architecture, landscapes
- Card crop: **3:2 landscape** · Hero: **full-bleed or 16:9**
